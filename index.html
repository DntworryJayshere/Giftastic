Animal Search

This is a simple site that makes use of the GIPHY API.

When you click on an animal button, the page should grab 6 static, non-animated gif images from the GIPHY API and place them on the page. When you click one of the still GIPHY images, the gif should animate & when you click the gif again, it should stop playing.
